Yardtech

Yardtech-1 is a retro FPS game engine inspired by idTech and Goldsrc. Its goal is to deliver the spirit and look of Goldsrc using modern C++. It aims to keep most aspects consistent with Goldsrc. It is currently in the very early stages of development. View the source code on GitHub.
