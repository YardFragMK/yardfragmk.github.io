Goblet of Blood

Goblet of Blood is a fast-paced, story-driven FPS game set on a hostile moon base. As Korkutay, a lone survivor facing alien threats and supernatural anomalies, you must uncover the fate of the missing N-12 crew and destroy the cursed Goblet of Sovereignty. Each level offers intense storytelling and a descent into madness. Designed for single-player PC gaming with classic FPS influences, Goblet of Blood blends action, atmosphere, and mystery in a chilling sci-fi setting.



Sign of Evil

Sign of Evil is a multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) game, still in the concept stage, that aims to blend Quake 3 Arena and Counter-Strike gameplay, offering chaotic gameplay.
