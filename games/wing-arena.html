Wing Arena

A competitive space shooter with a retro FPS feel, where four teams face off in high-speed space battles. Players pilot advanced warships from the cockpit, maneuvering through distant solar systems using a full 6DOF movement system. Wing Arena is written entirely in C++ based on the Yardtech-1 engine and is currently in the early stages of development.
